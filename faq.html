Our Menu
Does Banza serve halal meat?
Yes. All meat one our menu is halal and verified by the relevant bodies. Our certificate is displayed at all our locations.
Are there any vegan options on your menu?
Yes. There are options for vegan cheese and vegetarian options on our menu.
General
Can I cancel an order?
Yes, provided the cancellation is made half an hour after it is made.
Can I cancel a booking?
Bookings for our restaurant can only be made 10 days before the date. After this period, the agreed booking fee will not be refunded.
Do you offer group discounts for booking?
We do not currently offer discounts but do run seasonal promotional campaigns. Ask a member of staff in store or contact us for more information.
